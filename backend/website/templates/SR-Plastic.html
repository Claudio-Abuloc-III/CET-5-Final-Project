Plastic

Plastic, a versatile synthetic material derived from polymers, has become an integral part of modern life due to its durability and adaptability. However, our overreliance on plastic has led to a pressing environmental crisis marked by pollution, ecosystem disruption, and long-lasting waste. To address this challenge, recycling emerges as a crucial solution, offering a sustainable approach to minimize environmental impact, conserve resources, and alleviate the detrimental effects of plastic waste.
